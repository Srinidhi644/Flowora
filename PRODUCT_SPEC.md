# Flowora - All-in-One Life Management App

> **Day. Work. Cooking.** — Your whole day, beautifully managed.

---

## 1. Core Daily Dashboard (Home Screen)

- **Smart Daily Overview** — Shows your day at a glance: schedule, top 3 tasks, meals planned, weather, energy level prediction.
- **Time Blocking Visualizer** — Drag-and-drop blocks for Work, Deep Work, Cooking, Exercise, Rest, etc.
- **Energy & Mood Tracker** — Quick log how you feel throughout the day + AI suggestions based on past patterns.
- **One-Tap Routines** — "Start My Day" / "End My Day" buttons to kick off or wind down with guided flows.

---

## 2. Task & Work Management

- **To-Do Lists** with Eisenhower Matrix (Urgent/Important).
- **Project Workspaces** (for work) with subtasks, deadlines, and dependencies.
- **Pomodoro + Focus Timer** built-in with auto time blocking.
- **Meeting Scheduler** + auto-prep notes (e.g., "Meeting with Client X at 3 PM — agenda attached").
- **Work vs Personal Mode** toggle.
- **Weekly/Monthly Review** summaries with insights.

---

## 3. Meal Planning & Cooking Hub

> *This is the differentiator.*

### Recipe Manager
- Save your own recipes + import from web.

### Smart Meal Planner
Suggests weekly meals based on:
- Available ingredients in your fridge (user inputs or scans).
- Dietary preferences, allergies, calorie goals.
- Cooking time available (e.g., "I only have 30 mins today").
- Leftover management.

### Smart Shopping List
- Auto-generated from meal plan + manual additions.
- Sort by store aisle.

### Cooking Mode
- Step-by-step guided cooking with timer, voice commands, and progress tracker.

### Inventory Tracker
- Fridge/Pantry scanner (using camera) to track expiry dates and low stock alerts.

### "What Can I Cook Today?"
- Shows recipes based on what you currently have.

---

## 4. Advanced Integrations & AI Features

- **AI Daily Planner** — Tell it once ("I have 8 hours work, want to cook dinner, gym 3x/week"), and it builds your schedule.
- **Habit Tracker** — Water, exercise, reading, meditation, sleep — with streaks and reminders.
- **Finance Quick View** — Track daily expenses (especially grocery/cooking costs).
- **Location-Based Reminders** — "You're near supermarket — check shopping list?"
- **Voice Input** — Full voice control for adding tasks, recipes, logging meals.

## 5. Other Life Areas

- **Household Management** — Cleaning schedule, bill reminders, maintenance tasks.
- **Fitness & Health** — Workout planner, integrate with wearables (steps, calories).
- **Learning/Reading** — Track books, courses, skill development.
- **Digital Declutter** — App usage insights, focus mode.
- **Family/Shared Mode** — Share meal plans or shopping lists with partner/family.

## 6. Premium Features

- Recipe video integration or AR cooking guidance.
- Grocery price comparison across local stores.
- AI recipe generator (give it ingredients, get a custom recipe).
- Wearable integration (Oura, Apple Watch, Fitbit, or basic Android).
- Dark mode, beautiful minimal UI with festival/regional themes.
- Backup & Sync across phone + tablet.

## Tech Stack Recommendations

| Layer | Technology | Rationale |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Frontend | Flutter | Cross-platform, great on low-end Android |
| Backend | Firebase or Supabase | Fast & cheap to start |
| AI | Claude / Gemini / Grok API | Smart planning and recipe suggestions |
| Offline | Local-first architecture | Works without internet connectivity |
